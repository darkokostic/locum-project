Who we are

LocumOD was born out of the question, how are practices managing OD time away from the business? Experience of European markets and other medically related professions showed that Locums are used to great effect in providing continuing care and revenue.

Owned and operated by Ash Edwards, a 25 year veteran in Retail and Optical Retail,with experience in all levels of business operations, supply chain and support. Managing stores and supporting in administrative, management and director roles.

All that was missing in Canada was an easy to use, effective way of finding Locum professionals and corresponding Practice Vacancies. We set about building this service to bring together flexible Doctors of Optometry and innovative Practices wishing to deliver continual availability to their patients.

What we do

We are a service company born out of a unique view of the marketplace in Retail Optometry. In a changing landscape where efficiency rules and patient flexibility is key, we offer a way for practice to deliver a continuous service in the event of sickness, vacation and short term absence.

Practicing as a locum can offer an alternative way for Doctors of Optometry to utilize their skills and provides flexible hours, competitive rates, travel across Canada and exposure to varied practice environments. All with you in control. Our approach is to accurately represent all aspects of the Practice and Locum Doctors to ensure that a perfect match is achieved with each engagement. Our site offers easy to use geolocation, experience and specialty matching on key criteria, handling the booking, communication and invoicing.

LocumOD can drive revenue and maintain patient care even when the serving Doctor is away. In many cases this can account for thousands of dollars a day.

How are you managing vacation and time away from Practice?

Most practices will see an inevitable dip in revenue and productivity when the Doctors take vacation. Without eye examinations and Dr recommendations to drive patient purchases, retail activity falls to collections, outside Rx and adjustments. Bookings and recalls are pushed to when the Dr returns creating a wave of overbooked days.

Imagine having the flexibility to be “available” and generating peak revenue while you are away? That’s what we are here for.
